About Me

Hi, I'm Vijay Kumar, a passionate and enthusiastic BCA student at CT University, currently building my career in the fields of software development, web development, and AI/ML. Ever since I completed my schooling in Ludhiana, Punjab (originally from Bihar), I've been deeply interested in coding, technology, and creating impactful digital experiences.

As a fresher, I’m constantly learning and practicing skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, PHP, C++, Java, and exploring the exciting world of AI/ML. I’ve worked on projects like student attendance systems with location verification, secure login systems with OTP authentication, and even full-fledged gaming and classroom-based web apps.

I love solving real-world problems through tech and aim to contribute meaningfully to every team or company I join. I’m also improving my communication and English-speaking skills to become a well-rounded tech professional.
